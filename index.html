About Us

Welcome to the Church of Christ. We are a community of believers devoted to following the teachings of Jesus Christ and the example of the New Testament church.

Our mission is to worship God in spirit and in truth, study His Word, share the gospel, and serve one another in love.

  • The authority of the Bible as God’s Word
  • Baptism by immersion for the forgiveness of sins
  • Weekly observance of the Lord’s Supper
  • Simple, heartfelt worship without instruments

“Everyone is welcome to join us in worship, study, and fellowship.”
